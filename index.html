Welcome to THE HUMAN GENOME

THE HUMAN GENOME is a next-generation platform in reproductive genetics.

It empowers prospective parents undergoing IVF to make informed decisions by leveraging advanced genomic analysis. THE HUMAN GENOME enables comprehensive DNA screening of embryos before implantation, identifying risks for over 900 inherited conditions ‚Äî including cancer, cardiovascular diseases, neurological disorders, and more.

Key features of THE HUMAN GENOME:

  • Simultaneous screening of up to 20 embryos.
  • Genetic insights into health, longevity, mental well-being, and cognitive potential.
  • Actionable reports to guide the selection of the most viable and healthy embryo.

The mission of THE HUMAN GENOME is to improve the quality of life for future generations by minimizing genetic risks and offering a personalized, science-driven approach to family planning.
